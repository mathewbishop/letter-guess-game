Hacker

2077

The year is 2077. Corporations have replaced governments and capitalism has eclipsed democracy. The last few decades have seen the privitization of all things, and information sells at a premium. You are a hacker working for a group of benevolent subverters. You must hack into the servers of Sentinel Corp. and find information linking them to nefarious activities. Before evil can be defeated, it must be exposed to the light.

You must guess the correct letter to hack into Sentinel Corp. Each round you have 10 guesses. Achieve three 'Breakthroughs' to gain the information you need. If you fail to break through four times, Sentinel will have tracked your location. If this happens, wipe your hard drive, throw away your computer and run. The terminal prompt '>_' will display the letters you have already guessed this round. Guess a letter to begin.
